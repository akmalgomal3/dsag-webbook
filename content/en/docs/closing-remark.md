## Lessons Learned

Principles learned:

- **Trade-offs dominate.** No best structure exists: only constraints. Speed vs space. Simple vs optimal. Deterministic vs probabilistic.
- **Hardware impacts.** Cache, memory, GC pressure define real-world performance. Theoretical Big-O often fails to account for constants.
- **Go pragmatism.** Language favors simplicity and composition. Stdlib (`container/heap`, `sort`, `sync`) solves common tasks.
- **Patterns endure.** Algorithms accumulate human wisdom. History prevents reinvention.
